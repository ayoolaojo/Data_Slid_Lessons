TOP 5 BEST PHONES IN THE WORLD RIGHT NOW!

Phone 1

Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra

The S25 Ultra looks almost identical to the S24 Ultra, and from a hardware perspective, it is. But this doesn't stop it from being the best phone you can buy right now, provided money is no object. There are now even fewer weaknesses than before, with even more processing power, small but noticeable camera improvements.

Phone 2

One Plus 13

The OnePlus 13 takes what made the OnePlus 12 so great and makes it even better. This is the best phone OnePlus has ever made, and up there with the finest around right now. What makes it so good? It's a brilliant combination of top-class hardware, slick software and extensive support, meaning the phone could be your main phone for many years to come.

Phone 3

Xiaomi 14 Ultra

If photography is your top priority, there's no better phone than the Xiaomi 15 Ultra right now. When it comes to stills, there's no handset that does it better. Alongside a trio of 50Mp lenses (main, ultrawide and 3x telephoto), you also now get a 200Mp periscope with 4.3x optical zoom. That enables some of the best zoom shots you'll find on any phone.

Phone 4

Oppo Find X8 Pro

After Oppo decided not to release its Find X6 and Find X7 series flagships outside China, the Find X8 series marks a triumphant return to Europe. Both the Find X8 and Find X8 Pro are excellent phones, but the latter has a slight upper hand. It's powerful, good-looking, offers amazing battery life and has plenty of software perks.

Phone 5

Google Pixel 9 Pro XL

If none of the options above appeal to you, a Pixel 9 is probably your best bet. And if you want the absolute best experience, the newly-branded Pixel 9 Pro XL is the one to go for. While not quite the standout phone we saw with the Pixel 8 Pro, the 9 Pro XL still delivers a compelling experience in almost all key areas.
